Refund Policy

Last updated:

1. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard. When you cancel:

  • Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period
  • You will continue to have access to the Service and your remaining credits
  • No further charges will be made after the current period ends
  • You will not receive a refund for the current billing period

2. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds in the following circumstances:

2.1 Service Failure

If the Service is unavailable or fails to function for an extended period (more than 48 hours) due to our fault, you may be eligible for a partial or full refund of the affected billing period.

2.2 Duplicate Charges

If you are charged multiple times for the same subscription period, we will refund the duplicate charges immediately upon verification.

2.3 Billing Errors

If you are charged incorrectly due to a billing error on our part, we will refund the incorrect amount.

3. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Used credits - Credits that have been consumed through image processing cannot be refunded
  • Partial billing periods - No prorated refunds for partial months
  • Change of mind - Refunds are not available simply because you no longer wish to use the Service
  • Unsatisfactory results - We cannot guarantee specific AI processing results, and refunds are not available based on output quality

4. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact us through your account dashboard or via the contact information on our website
  2. Provide your account email and subscription details
  3. Explain the reason for your refund request
  4. We will review your request within 5-7 business days
  5. If approved, refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 10-14 business days

5. Refund Timeline

Refund processing times vary by payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days after processing
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days after processing
  • Other Payment Methods: 10-14 business days after processing

Note: The actual time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your bank or payment provider.

6. Chargebacks

If you file a chargeback or dispute with your payment provider instead of contacting us first, we may suspend your account pending resolution. We encourage you to contact us directly to resolve any billing issues.

7. Special Circumstances

We understand that exceptional circumstances may arise. If you believe your situation warrants special consideration, please contact us with details, and we will review your case on an individual basis.

8. Changes to Refund Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact for Refunds

For refund requests or questions about this policy, please contact us through your account dashboard or via the contact information provided on our website.
