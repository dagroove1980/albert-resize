Frequently Asked Questions

What is a credit?

Each credit equals one image processing. When you upload an image and extend it to a new size, one credit is used.

Do credits roll over?

Credits reset each month when your subscription renews. Unused credits from the previous month do not carry over.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and debit cards through our secure payment processor.

What image sizes can I extend to?

You can extend images to Square (1024×1024), Portrait (1080×1920 or 1024×1792), or Landscape (1792×1024) formats.
